WHATSAPP GROUP QR CODE INSTRUCTIONS
====================================

Please add your WhatsApp group QR code image to this folder with the filename:
  Group.jpeg

Steps to get your WhatsApp group QR code:
1. Open WhatsApp and go to your group
2. Tap on the group name at the top
3. Tap "Invite via link"
4. Tap "QR code"
5. Save the QR code image
6. Rename it to "Group.jpeg"
7. Place it in this folder: Hackathon-Master-Plan/artifacts/hackathon/public/

The image will automatically appear on the registration success page.

Supported formats: Group.jpeg, Group.jpg, or Group.png
